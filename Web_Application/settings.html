    About SavorShelf

    SavorShelf is a cutting-edge pantry management system designed to eliminate food waste through intelligent AI vision.

    AI Expiry Detection
    Real-time Freshness Monitoring
    Smart Grocery Insights

    Our mission is to empower households to make smarter consumption choices, saving money and the environment.

    Privacy Policy

    Your Privacy Matters. At SavorShelf, we prioritize the protection of your personal and inventory data.

    1. We only collect data necessary for expiry tracking.

    2. Your images are processed securely and never shared with third parties for advertising.

    3. All account data is encrypted using industry-standard protocols.

    We believe in full transparency. Your data belongs to you, and you can export or delete it at any time.

    Terms & Conditions

    By using SavorShelf, you agree to our platform standards and inventory protocols.

    • Accuracy: While our AI is highly accurate, users should always verify critical expiry dates manually.

    • Usage: This platform is intended for personal kitchen management only.

    • Data: We retain your inventory history to provide freshness analytics until you choose to delete your account.

    Danger Zone

    Deleting your account is permanent. All your products, scanned history, and settings will be wiped instantly.
